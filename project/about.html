About the Master

Maria Llivicura is an Ecuadorian women who opened up her shop in 2020.She has always had a passion for shoe shine and repair and learned from a young age how to take care of shoes. According to her, shoes hold many memories and is important to hold onto. Growing up in Ecuador, she learned that shoes, bags, etc. can never be thrown out, it should be repaired or reused for something else. This mindset is what drives her to meet her clients expectations and needs.

Maria Llivicura is a versatile woman who conquers the world of shoeshine and truly values her customers and their support. She immigrated from Ecuador more than 20 years ago and has worked in the world of show shine for 23 years. Her passion for shoes, purses, and many more services was why she decided to open a small shop in 2020. While being a full-time mom and having suffered due to the pandemic and loss of revenue. She has come back stronger and pleased all her customers.
